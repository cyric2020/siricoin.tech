A short story on the birth of Siricoin

We - Siricoin devs first met up on a crypto currency discord server
and we didnt know much about crypto at the time and didnt know how to
code ¯\_(ツ)_/¯ one fine day we all got banned from that discord server,
from there onwards we took matters into our own hands and decided to make our own crypto currency.

Since we didn't know how to code and make cryptos we looking up
different cryptos that can be mined on android phones and avrs, but we couldnt find
any of them which were decentralized. So we learnt Coding and came to a final decision
of making Siricoin which can be mined on low powered devices :)

What is SiriCoin?
SiriCoin is a lightweight crypto which can be mined through low-powered devices like CPU's, GPU's microcontroller's such as Arduinos and ESP's, RPI's and Mobile devices.
